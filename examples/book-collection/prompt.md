# BookShelf - Personal Library Management System

Build a comprehensive book collection manager for book lovers who want to catalog, discover, and manage their personal library. Features should rival commercial apps but keep everything local and private.

## Core Features

### Collection Management

- **ISBN Scanning**: Scan ISBN barcodes with camera for quick book entry
- **Manual Entry**: Add books with title, author, and other relevant details
- **Book Covers**: Fetch covers from online sources or upload custom images with adjustment controls
- **Multiple Formats**: Track physical books, ebooks, and audiobooks
- **Conditions**: Track book condition for your collection
- **Edition Tracking**: Track specific editions and multiple copies
- **Acquisition**: Record when, where, and at what price books were acquired
- **Beautiful Library**: Visual display with cover art in various view modes, smooth animations, and themes

### Reading Progress

- **Reading Status**: Track books across statuses: want to read, currently reading, read, abandoned, on hold
- **Progress Tracking**: Update and view reading progress with pages read, percentage complete
- **Reading Streaks**: Track consistent reading habits over time

### Notes & Comments

- **Book Comments**: Leave notes and comments on individual books
- **Reviews**: Write and store book reviews with ratings

### Shelves & Organization

- **Custom Shelves**: Create unlimited custom shelves for different categories
- **Smart Shelves**: Auto-populate shelves based on configurable rules
- **Sorting**: Sort by title, author, rating, date added, date read, or progress
- **Filtering**: Filter collection by genre, author, format, rating, status, or tags
- **Tags**: Flexible tagging system for themes, mood, setting, or time period

### Statistics & Insights

- **Library Stats**: Overview of total books, pages, genres, and authors
- **Reading Stats**: Track books read over time with detailed statistics
- **Reading Trends**: Visualize reading patterns over months and years
- **Genre Distribution**: Charts showing breakdown of genres in your collection
- **Reading Pace**: Track pages read over various time periods
- **Achievements**: Badges or milestones for reading accomplishments
- **Year in Review**: Annual reading summary with meaningful insights

### Import/Export

- **Import**: Import from CSV, JSON, or exports from other services
- **Export**: Export collection to various formats for backup or migration
- **Backup**: Full database backup capability
- **Selective Export**: Export portions of collection by shelf, genre, or date range

## Success Criteria

- **Easy Entry**: Add books quickly with minimal friction
- **Fast Capture of Books** using the device camera to scan ISBN codes and fetch metadata
- **Rich Data**: Fetch covers, descriptions, and metadata automatically when available
- **Beautiful Library**: Grid view with covers looks visually appealing
- **Insightful Stats**: Statistics feel meaningful and provide real value
- **Works Offline**: Core functionality available without internet connection
- **Import/Export**: Easy migration from other services
- **Private**: All data local, no account required
- **Mobile Friendly**: Usable on mobile devices for cataloging at bookstores
- **Tested**: Core functionality validated with automated tests

## Bonus Features

- Reading quotes feature from your collection
